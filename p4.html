Project #4 Experimental Clock

Date:

3/4/24

Medium:

P5 Code

Decription:

Clock based on seasons!

Design Process:

This clock keeps track of time bsaed on the changing of the seasons! As each season comes in, it's represented by the changing colors of the grass grown. As each color change happens, it represents the passing of time. I was struggling on which sketch to go off of. I spent some time with my friends outside which inspired this clock. I noticed how the passing of time can come through the smaller moments. As a result I thought about the grass and how its slow growth shows time passing despite its gradual process. That's how I came to focus on the idea of grass and seasonal changes.
